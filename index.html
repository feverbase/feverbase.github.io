Supporting research to fight COVID-19.

We're creating a simple website as a landing point to collect data from COVID doctors around the world to learn about the quality of their data. This is being run by Laura Deming, Ed Boyden, and Jan Liphardt.

The first step is to create a decent looking landing page with text I can write about what we're asking for, and an email account private@feverbase.com that can receive emails with accounts of different treatments for different patients and the outcome. I'll manage the inbox.
